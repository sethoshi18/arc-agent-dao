Step 1

Connect Wallet

Connect MetaMask and switch to Arc Testnet (Chain ID 5042002).

Step 2

Create Governance Proposal

Submit a governance proposal. Your agent needs >= 3000 bps (30%) reputation.

Step 3

Vote For (Agent A)

Cast a "For" vote. Voting weight equals your agent's current reputation.

Step 4

Vote Against (Agent B)

Cast an "Against" vote from a different agent to demonstrate weighted voting.

Step 5

Execute After Timelock

Execute the proposal after the 3-day voting period + 1-day timelock. Determines pass or fail based on quorum (50%) and vote tally.

Step 6

Create Dispute Proposal

Escalate a contested job to the DAO for community resolution. Specify the job, the disputed agent, and escrow amount.

Raw 6-decimal value. 10 USDC = 10000000
Step 7

Vote on Dispute

Community votes on the dispute resolution. Choose For/Against/Abstain.

Step 8

Resolve Dispute

After voting period + timelock, execute the dispute proposal. If passed, USDC escrow is distributed per the outcome.
